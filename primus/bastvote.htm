The Bastard Vote

Here the chance for you to choose your top 10 Primus, Les Claypool, and Sausage Songs of all time! Don't worry too much about getting things in exact order: if your tastes change, just come back and vote again!

Instructions: This JavaScript form will only work under Netscape 4.0 and (for the most part) Internet Explorer 3. People with other browsers should use the less convienient Standard Voting Form.

  1. Choose your favorite songs by clicking on the pull-down menus at the right. The radio button shows which place song you are selecting.
  2. A first place vote gets 10 points, a second place vote gets 9 points, etc.
  3. If you want to change a selection, click on the radio button next to the song you want to change.
  4. When you're done, hit the 'Submit' button.
