Tabbed by Aaron Pence. 

The tab above is wrong. Not that it's actually incorrect, for it's
nearly exactly right - for guitar. The problem is that the guitar
handles the main line in the verses of this song. The bass does not
play this part out until the chorus, where it's incredibly low. The
intro looks a lot like this, en tab.

All notes in this are tapped;
C:-5/10--10-10-10-10-9-9-9-11--|-5/10--10-10-10-10-9-9-9-11--|
G:-5/10--10-10-10-10-9-9-9-11--|-5/10--10-10-10-10-9-9-9-11--|

The general idea is just to tap out the fourths in these three frets,
and try to follow the pattern that Monsieur Claypool established. It's
not so hard. If you have a four string, be a decent, moral human being
and just add five to all the frets above. Simple.
