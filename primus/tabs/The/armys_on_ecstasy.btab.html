I saw the live video (I think it's on the Oysterhead website) and I'm
pretty sure this is correct. I've got it in two parts: you play the
first half, and then the second half and it repeats. The first half is
pretty much the same every time but the second half is different in
the intro. Of course, it changes a little here and there.

1st measure...
   1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a

play the 5's, but it doesn't sound as good.

second measure all of these superfast slappy-poppy things like the
ones in Laquerhead and Kalamazoo.
