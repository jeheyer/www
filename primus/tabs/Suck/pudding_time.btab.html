Primus - Pudding Time

Comment by Doug Aamoth. 

I played this song using this tab and it didn't sound quite right so I
came up with what I thought sounded more like the actual song.  In the
intro, I found that of hitting 1-3-3-3 on the D and G strings, 7- 9
sounded better.  I also figured out the magic part at the end (humpty
dumpty sat on a wall).  It is: 13-16, 13-16, 13-16, 13-16-13- 11, and
then the part Les changes goes: 13-16, 13-16, 19--18 all on the G
string.
