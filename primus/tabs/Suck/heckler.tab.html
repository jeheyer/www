Tabbed by Scott Martin. 

This song is not that hard to play, but boy oh boy, you people who
submit tabs to the Cheesy Primus Page must be nuts. It is played on a
4-stringer.  I saw Les play this on the Punchbowl tour, and if any of
you disagree, please download the heckler .au file from
http://www.mediacast.com in the Primus folder.
