Primus - The Antipop

Tabbed by Matt Wilkinson. 

Sampler version.  For all of you lucky enough to get your
bass-blistered fingers on Primus' new sampler "The Antipop", here is
the medly down on tab.  This new set of songs shows a totally new side
of Primus (this would be side number 9 I believe).  Like always, I'm
too lazy to tab the thing note-by-note, so just put the pieces
together.  I might put the whole thing together someday. Have fun!

Key
----------------------------------
v= downstroke of flemenco
^= upstroke of flemenco
h= hammer-on
po = pull-off
s= slap
p= pop
() = optional, or hardly noticable in the the music.
