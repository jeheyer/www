Here's some help if you don't understand Riff 1.  First of all the
easisest way to understand that riff is to count the 27/16 sequence as
8 + 8 + 8 + 3 16th notes. This figure might also help you:

1234 5678 1234 5678 |       |
1234 5678 12*1 2345 |       |
6781 2345 6781 2345 | intro | chorus
6781 2*12 3456 7812 |       |
3456 7812 3456 7812 |       |
*123 4567 8123 4567 |
8123 4567 812* 1234 |
5678 1234 5678 1234 |
5678 12*1 2345 6781 |
2345 6781 2345 6781 |
2*12 3456 7812 3456 |
7812 3456 7812      |

The last notes of the sequence are marked with a *. The intro is 11
4/4 bars and 1 3/4 bar. The chorus is the first 5 4/4 bars.
