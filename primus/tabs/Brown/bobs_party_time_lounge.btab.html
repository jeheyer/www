Explanation of cool part: Okay the cool part is tapped. To do it, rest
both hands over the fret board. The first note (16) is tapped with the
middle finger of your right hand. The next two notes are hammered on
with the first and second (index and middle) fingers of your right
hand.  Then the last note is played with the index finger of you right
hand. You have to hit the notes hard and fast, and it takes a bit of
practice, but it's the only way to get the sound that Les gets.  Oh
yeah, for lefties, just switch the hands around, right becomes left,
left becomes right.
