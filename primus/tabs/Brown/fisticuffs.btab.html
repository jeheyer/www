Primus - Fisticuffs

Tabbed by SyberOptic (SyberOptic@aol.com). 

   S = slap
   P = pop
   v / ^  = strum; downstroke/upstroke
   x = muted
   /  = slide
   h = hammer-on
   p = pull-off
   r  = rest

The 2h4 hammer-ons are played fast as to only be one beat each.  The
mutes are 1/2 beat each. All notes are plucked normally with the
fingers, but it sounds cool played slap too.

    play 3 times
