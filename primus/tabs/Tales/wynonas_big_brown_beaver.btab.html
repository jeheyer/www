for fret 12 just play muted but not over the medal. The . in between
the 12 so you won't think it's 1212121212. Play as a mute.

Primus - Wynona's Big Brown Beaver

Tabbed by Nathan. 

Les is the man!  This song gives me wood.

Since Les uses some flamenco style, he does a little quadruplet by quickly
shooting out 3 fingers and then hitting the last note.  I forget the proper
terminology, but it sounds like "bababa DA."  You'll recognize it when you
hear the song.  Anyway, for the purposes of this tab, this quadruplet is
designated with a /\.  What I do is shoot out my ring, middle, and index
finger, then hit the last note with an upstroke of my thumb nail.
