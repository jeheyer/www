Interludes: Les just fools around on the 9th and 11th frets on the D,
G, B, and e strings and it's really close sounding to the intro
part. He's basically making stuff up for the interludes. Make up your
own interludes. Get your creative juices flowing. Just make sure they
go with the song and are somewhere on the 9th and 11th frets on the D,
G, B, and e strings.
