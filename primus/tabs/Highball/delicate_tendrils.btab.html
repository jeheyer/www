Les Claypool and the Holy Mackerel - Delicate Tendrils

Tabbed by Erik Elden. 

This is a seemlingly easy song to play, but it isn't easy to play it
with Les' right-on-the-beat rythm and perfection.  Played on Les'
6-string fretted Carl Thompson with a fat sound. All notes are slapped
unless marked otherwise.

h = hammer-on
