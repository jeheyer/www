Holy Mackerel main riff (unless you've got a five string you have to
detune you're E string to a D flat):

  A:|-----------------------4--------------|
 Db:|--0--2--3/0----0-----0--2--3/0--------|

All notes here are slapped except for the D flat on the A-string
(fourth frett) which is plucked.

Chorus:

  A:|-----------------------|
 Db:|---8---X-11---X-5------| Holy Mackerel!

I don't have the intro yet, I'm pretty sure it's just a variation of the
chorus, maybe an open Db is hit before the 8th fret and 11th fret.
