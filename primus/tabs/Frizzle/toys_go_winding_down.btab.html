Tabbed by Standard. 


Here's the correct bass tab for Toys Go Winding Down.

t= play the note as a triplet (flick the first three fingers of your
   right hand on the string)
h= hammer on/off
d= play the string with both fingers at once to produce a full sound

A -0t-0t-----0t-0t-3t-0t------0t-0t------0t-3t-2t-1t---------------

and repeat.
