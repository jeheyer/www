As with all the other tabs, it cannot be stressed enough that you need
to treat this as a mere statistical average. It's not completely
intractable - you've got to play with it, or it will NEVER sound right.
Not only am I way too careless to toss in all the fills and glances,
but they'd be stupid to write out - you've got to be able to lay out
your own little feel for it. If you can't do that yet, don't despair.
Just practice more. You know the drill. Best o' luck.
