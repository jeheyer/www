Primus - Groundhog's Day

Tabbed by Aaron Pence.

Key; HH:Hi-hat//SD:Snare Drum//BD:Bass Drum//CC:Crash Cymbal//RB:Ride
Bell//RS:Ride Sheet(the part that isn't the bell)//T1:Highest Tom-tom.
_______________________________________________________________________
The start of this song is either in 3/4 or 4/4-with-a-triplet-base time.
It doesn't matter which, because they end up being the same pragmatic
thing, but the important note is that you recognize - and actually
play - it in this fashion. The problem with tab is that you can't
really show time without making it any more illegible than it already
is.
