Here is "Groundhog's Day".  I really don't feel like tabbing out the
whole song, so I'll just throw the different riffs at you, and you can
figure it out for yourself.

The Chords: Les plays about 50,000 different variations to the main
riff.  Just kinda mix it up.  You'll be able to hear when Les makes
the chord changes.  When you stum, use that good 'ol flemenco, but
only use your index finger-- it gives it a less "powerfull"
sound--much cleaner.
