Tabbed by Jeffrey Swing (js018d@uhura.cc.rochester.edu ).

Notes on notation: Since flamenco style strumming is a big part of Les Claypool's style, I will denote a down-stroke strum (played by strumming with your picking hand fingernails) with a "V", and an up-stroke strum (you can use your thumbnail, or the pads of your fingertips) with a "^". I will try to stick to the faq for the rest of the notation, (or at least make it understandable). Also, I will indicate vibrato by writing "/\/\/\/\/\/\/" above the staff. Triplets will be indicated by "|-3-|" also above the staff, the triplets shoud be played with three fingers, starting with your ring finger (that's the easiest way I've found to play them).
