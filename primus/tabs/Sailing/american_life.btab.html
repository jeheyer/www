Primus - American Life

Tabbed by Chris Norwood. 

I noticed that all the tabs for American Life are wrong, most likely
including this one, and I wanted to get Primus fans one step closer to
the true tab.  The most important thing needed to know when playing
this song is the rythym.  In my tab, the 3 on the E string and the 0s
on the A string make up the base rythym. Between the 3 and the first
0, the notes are triplets. In the second space, they are double timed
triplets.

P-pull off     H- hammer on     S-slap
                          {Double Time}
