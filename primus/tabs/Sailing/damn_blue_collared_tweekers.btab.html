Bass Solo:

Use Jeffery Swing's tab for the solo, most of it is accurate.

When Les uses his whammy at the part "These damn blue collar tweekers,
they've always run this town", you can use your tuning key for your E
string and just tune down and then back up.

Octave part at the end:

G --14-16-------------------------|
D --14-16-------------------------|
A --------------------------------|
E --------0-/-14--0-/-14--0-/14---|

Instead of 2-4-2-6-4-2-4-2-4, use 14-16-14/-18-16-14-16-14-16 for the
chord part.
