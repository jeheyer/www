A--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
E-----0--------------------------------------------------------------------------

 G----9---9---9---9/10--10\9
 D----9---9---9---9/10--10\9

sliding up yet once more to the 4th fret on the d and the 6th fret on
the g string.
