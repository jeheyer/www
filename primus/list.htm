The Bastard List

The Bastards List is for internet-specific discussion about Primus: IRC, Cheesy Chat, Cheesy Ring, etc. It is recommended for people with Primus Web Pages, although anyone with interest is free to join.

To subscribe, send an e-mail to majordomo@jfive.com with the words "subscribe bastards" (no quotes) in the body of the message. Or, better yet, just fill out this form:

Action:
Subscribe Unsubscribe

Once you've subscribed to the list, send messages to bastards@jfive.com.
